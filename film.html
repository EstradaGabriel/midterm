My TV Shows

The Dark Knight

When the menace known as the Joker emerges from his mysterious past, he wreaks havoc and chaos on the people of Gotham. The Dark Knight must accept one of the greatest psychological and physical tests of his ability to fight injustice.

  • Director: Christopher Nolan
  • Cast: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart
  • Genres: Action, Crime, Drama
  • Release Year: 2008
  • Rating: 9.0/10

The Intern

Seventy-year-old widower Ben Whittaker has discovered that retirement isn't all it's cracked up to be. Seizing an opportunity to get back in the game, he becomes a senior intern at an online fashion site, founded and run by Jules Ostin.

  • Director: Nancy Meyers
  • Cast: Robert De Niro, Anne Hathaway, Rene Russo
  • Genres: Comedy, Drama
  • Release Year: 2015
  • Rating: 7.1/10

How to Train Your Dragon

A hapless young Viking who aspires to hunt dragons becomes the unlikely friend of a young dragon himself, and learns there may be more to the creatures than he assumed.

  • Directors: Dean DeBlois, Chris Sanders
  • Cast: Jay Baruchel, Gerard Butler, Christopher Mintz-Plasse
  • Genres: Animation, Action, Adventure
  • Release Year: 2010
  • Rating: 8.1/10

Cars 2

Star race car Lightning McQueen and his pal Mater head overseas to compete in the World Grand Prix, but the road to the championship becomes rocky as Mater gets caught up in international espionage.

  • Directors: John Lasseter, Brad Lewis
  • Cast: Owen Wilson, Larry the Cable Guy, Michael Caine
  • Genres: Animation, Adventure, Comedy
  • Release Year: 2011
  • Rating: 6.2/10
